About Fitness Tracker

Our Mission

Fitness Tracker is designed to help you monitor your exercise routine and track your progress over time. Our goal is to provide a simple, intuitive tool that helps you stay motivated and achieve your fitness goals.

Features

  • Record your exercises with details
  • Track duration and calories burned
  • Monitor your progress with visual charts
  • Edit or delete previous entries
